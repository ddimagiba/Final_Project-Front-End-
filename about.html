Bio With over 15 years of DJing experince, DJ Sickroc has established himself a balance of party rocking and turntablism in the New York City club scene. Starting out as battle DJ entering numerous competitions such as the DMC and ITF, Sickroc also gained notoriety spinning and throwing BBoy Jams where he gained his knowledge on breaks. He also learned the art of party rocking in college, doing house parties and local clubs. Now established in the New York City club scene he has spun at many New York hot spots such as Hudson Hotel, Club Touch, 675 Lounge, Le Poisson Rouge, The Brickwood, Tenjune, Griffen and Katra to name a few. As a member of The Elite Camp, he is able to mix diverse styles of music all in one night ranging from, 80s to house, funk to rock, hip-hop to EDM.
